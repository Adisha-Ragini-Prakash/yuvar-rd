    🛤️ Patent Filing Pathway

    1️⃣

    Provisional Patent Application

    File immediately to secure your filing date. Valid for 12 months.

    Cost: ₹1,600 (for individuals) | Timeline: 2-3 days

    2️⃣

    Complete Specification

    Submit detailed patent application within 12 months of provisional filing.

    Cost: ₹8,000 (for individuals) | Timeline: Within 12 months

    3️⃣

    Examination Request

    Request examination of your patent application.

    Cost: ₹8,000 (for individuals) | Timeline: Within 48 months

    4️⃣

    Patent Grant

    Receive patent certificate after successful examination.

    Timeline: 2-3 years from filing | Protection: 20 years
